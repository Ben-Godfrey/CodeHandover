1. What can I ask about?

In it's current form, the chatbot mostly knows about univeristy societies, campus locations, and popular nightlife venues. You can ask for addresses and descriptions (eg. what and where type questions). In the case of societies, you an also ask about fees and where to sign up.

2. Are any of my responses recorded?

Our chatbot will try it's best to remember what you are talking about during your coversation. No information will be stored any longer than it needs to be.

3. I asked the chatbot a question and it didn't respond, what's wrong?

Sometimes the chatbot may stumble with certain sentences. Don't worry, this isn't a crash. Try rephrasing your question and hopefully you receive a more appropriate response.

4. Does the chatbot store my location data?

No data like this is stored by the chatbot. The map function exists as a frame of reference and is centered on the Diamond.

5. Does the chatbot have a name?

While it is very cute, our chatbot is currently unnamed. Feel free to send any suggestions to bgodfrey2@sheffield.ac.uk
