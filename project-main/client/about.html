About Us

Welcome to our society's chatbot designed primarily for first-year university students! We are a team of six enthusiastic students from the prestigious University of Sheffield, working on behalf of our client IBM as part of an exciting AI learning project. Our goal is to create a chatbot that provides valuable information and assistance to new students navigating their first year of university. With our combined knowledge and expertise in artificial intelligence and programming, we are committed to building a user-friendly chatbot that will help students find answers to their questions and support them in their academic journey. We are proud to be part of this innovative project and look forward to serving the needs of our fellow university students.

About IBM

IBM, or International Business Machines Corporation, is a global technology company known for its innovation in areas such as hardware, software, cloud computing, AI, and quantum computing. With a legacy dating back to 1911, IBM has a strong reputation for pioneering groundbreaking technologies and providing advanced technology solutions to businesses and governments worldwide.
